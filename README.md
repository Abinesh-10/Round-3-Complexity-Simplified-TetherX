🚗 Cruise AutoCare
Smart Vehicle Maintenance & Service Tracking Platform

Cruise AutoCare is a digital platform that helps vehicle owners, mechanics, and administrators manage vehicle maintenance efficiently. The system records service history, tracks maintenance schedules, and provides alerts for upcoming or overdue servicing.

By centralizing vehicle data and maintenance records, Cruise AutoCare improves vehicle reliability, reduces unexpected breakdowns, and ensures timely servicing.

🎯 Problem

Vehicle maintenance is often tracked manually through service receipts or reminders. This leads to missed service schedules, poor record keeping, and unexpected vehicle failures.

💡 Solution

Cruise AutoCare provides a centralized system to register vehicles, maintain service records, and monitor maintenance schedules. The platform helps users manage maintenance activities while keeping a complete digital history of each vehicle.
